Why IT

I often compare programming with magic. How it is so?

The modern IT sorcerers use their magic altar, a PC with programming software, to create grimoires, the programs, which are full of spells, code pieces, to cast them in the virtual world. And not just that!

Decades ago, programming was a science more than of any application usage. But nowadays the power of this digital enchantment is so strong that it affects almost every area of human life.

Since my young age, I have been fascinated and inspired by the bright artificial worlds provided by video games. Later, I've also got amazed an unimaginable potential of the web connecting people across the globe with almost no limits. I dreamed about initiating in that art of technical mages.

Unfortunately, my life has turned the other way. My teen age attempts and experiments with game engines, small home websites and Adobe Flash technology (ActionScript 3.0) gave place to something more real and practical, like veterinary science. After getting my high education in that area, I decided to return to my dream.

Combining the work and the self-education, I learned programming basis and my first language, C#. The first steps were pretty humble, but the path of learning the IT art is a never-ending process.

Finally, I decided to change my life. It wasn't easy, taking into account the fact I don't have the specific education, which fuels imposter syndrome. But I made a firm decision it shouldn't stop me on the way to what I like and have been wanting to do all along.

Let this page be a declaration of the fresh start towards making my dream true!

Nightwrap

A minimalistic Windows desktop service which conceals the screen with black screensaver in a specified time interval if was no input detected. Made for users whose PC locks, logs out, or shuts down right after screen switches off.

Supports specifying the interval, working in the system tray, and loading on Windows startup.

GitHub repository

ReaderX

A Windows desktop app designed to display text over changeable background images which are dynamically loaded from the specified folder. Thus it combines reader and slideshow functions.

Also provides font formatting, installing random or consequential order of slideshow with the adjustable interval, selecting specific background from the list, and usage of hotkeys.
