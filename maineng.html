Why programming

I often compare programming with magic. How it is so? The modern IT mages use their magic altar, a PC with programming software, to create grimoirs of the programs, full of spells, which are structures, to induce the magic in the virtual world. And not just that. Unlike decades ago, when programming was a science more than of any application usage, nowadays the power of this magic is so strong that it affects almost every area of human life.

From my young age, I have been fascinated and inspired by the bright artificial worlds provided by video games, as well as unimaginable potential of the web connecting people. I dreamed about initiating in that art of technical mages.

Unfortunately, my life has turned the other way. My teen age attempts and experiments with game engines, small home websites and Adobe Flash technology (ActionScript 3.0) gave place to something more real and practical, like veterinary science. After getting my high education in that area, I decided to return to my dream. Combining the work and the self-education, I learned programming basis and my first programming language, C#. The first steps were pretty humble, but the path of learning the IT art is a never-ending process.

Finally, I decided to change my life. It wasn't easy, realizing the fact I don't have the specific education, which fuels imposter syndrome. This page is the start, the bright one I believe, towards making my dream true.
